Privacy Policy for MEDCROSS by TB Clinic

Operated by **UshaPrakash Health Care Private Limited**

UshaPrakash Health Care Private Limited ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We operate the **MEDCROSS App by TB Clinic** to provide healthcare services to individuals affected by **tuberculosis (TB)** and **diabetes**. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in simple language.

1. What We Collect

  • **Profile info** – name, age, mobile number, email you provide
  • **Health info** – TB or diabetes symptoms, lab results, and test reports you upload
  • **Device & usage** – phone model, crash logs, screens you tap

2. Why We Use It (Healthcare Purposes)

We use your data only to deliver and improve healthcare, specifically:

  • **Diseases & Conditions Management** – track TB and diabetes status and monitor progress
  • **Clinical Decision Support** – help doctors decide appropriate tests and treatments
  • **Disease Prevention & Public Health** – comply with TB reporting laws and create anonymized statistics
  • **Healthcare Services & Management** – register patients, book appointments, and maintain medical records
  • **Medication & Treatment Management** – share prescriptions with pharmacies, test orders with labs, send dosage reminders and alerts
  • **App Improvement & security** – fix bugs, measure app usage, prevent fraud
  • **Legal compliance** – fulfill obligations under Indian health laws

We **never sell** your data. We may share only the minimum required information with **partner pharmacies and diagnostic labs** to ensure your medicines are delivered and tests are conducted.

3. When We Share

WHO WHY
WHO To treat you effectively
Our doctors & counsellors To treat you effectively
Service providers (cloud, SMS, analytics) To operate and secure the app
Partner pharmacies & labs To dispense prescriptions and conduct tests
Government agencies Only if legally required

All third parties sign strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Your Choices

  • Request a **copy** of your personal data
  • **Edit or delete** your data (medical records are retained up to 8 years by law)
  • **Turn off analytics** in the app settings

Write to us, and we'll respond within 15 days.

5. Security & Storage

  • Data is encrypted during transfer (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Only authorized staff can access your data, and regular security reviews are conducted

6. Children

The MEDCROSS app is **not intended for users under 13 years**. Parents or guardians can contact us to have a child's data removed.
